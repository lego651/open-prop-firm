## Additional Details

- Refundable fee: Yes, refunded with the first successful payout from the funded account.
- Daily loss limit is calculated as a fixed percentage of the initial account balance, not trailing equity. It resets at 00:00 server time (GMT+3 during DST, GMT+2 otherwise).
- Swaps, commissions, and fees count toward the daily loss limit.
- Optional add-ons are available for the Stellar 2-Step (e.g., Lifetime Reward 95%, No Minimum Trading Days) at additional cost.

# Funded Next — Changelog

A history of notable changes to Funded Next's challenges, rules, and promotions. Most recent changes first.

## 2024 (approximate) — Stellar rebrand and product consolidation

Funded Next consolidated its challenge lineup under the "Stellar" brand, replacing earlier product names. The three-variant structure (Stellar 2-Step, Stellar 1-Step, Stellar Lite) was introduced, unifying pricing tiers and allowing traders to choose their preferred evaluation model at a single price point per account size.

Source: https://fundednext.com/stellar-model

---

## 2023 (approximate) — Stellar Instant product launched

Funded Next launched the Stellar Instant product, offering immediate funded account access without a challenge evaluation phase. The Instant product introduced a trailing max-loss drawdown model (6% from peak equity) rather than the static balance-based drawdown used in evaluation challenges. Account sizes ranged from $6,000 to $20,000, with a scaling mechanism that doubles allocation every 10% growth cycle up to $2,000,000.

Source: https://fundednext.com/stellar-instant

---

## 2022 — Company founded

GrowthNext F.Z.E. was incorporated in Ajman, UAE, and the Funded Next brand launched. The firm began offering CFD prop trading evaluation programs targeting retail traders in the Forex, commodities, and indices markets. Operations subsequently expanded to include entities in Comoros Islands, Hong Kong, and Cyprus to serve broader international markets.

Source: https://fundednext.com

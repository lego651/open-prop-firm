# Funding Pips — Changelog

A history of notable changes to Funding Pips's challenges, rules, and promotions. Most recent changes first.

## 2024–2025 (approximate) — Introduction of Zero and 2-Step Pro models

Funding Pips expanded its challenge lineup beyond the original 1-Step and 2-Step Classic to include the Zero (instant-funded) model and the 2-Step Pro variant. The Zero model provides immediate funded account access with tighter drawdown rules; the 2-Step Pro offers lower entry prices in exchange for reduced profit targets per phase (6%/6%) and stricter risk limits (3% daily, 6% overall).

_Verified as of March 2026._

---

## 2023 (approximate) — Payout split structure introduced

Funding Pips introduced the frequency-based payout split system: traders choosing weekly withdrawals receive 60% of profits, bi-weekly 80%, and monthly 100%. This model replaced any earlier fixed-split approach and allows traders to self-select between faster access to funds and a higher profit share.

_Verified as of March 2026._

---

## 2022 — Company founded

Funding Pips was founded in 2022 and launched its CFD prop trading evaluation platform from Dubai, UAE (IFZA Business Park). The firm initially offered a 2-Step evaluation model targeting Forex and CFD traders. Account sizes at launch ranged from $5,000 to $100,000.

_Verified as of March 2026._

# Funding Pips — Trading Rules

Funding Pips applies a uniform set of trading rules across all challenge models (Zero, 1-Step, 2-Step Classic, 2-Step Pro). Specific drawdown thresholds and profit targets differ by model — see individual challenge pages for those figures. The rules below describe the methodology and restrictions that apply to all models.

## Drawdown Rules

- **Daily drawdown:** The daily loss limit is calculated as a percentage of the initial account balance (balance-based, not equity-based). The daily loss calculation resets each day at the server's daily cutoff.
- **Overall drawdown:** Static (balance-based). The maximum drawdown threshold is calculated from the initial account balance and does not trail upward as the account grows. Limits range from 5% (Zero model) to 10% (2-Step Classic).
- **Drawdown resets:** No automatic reset mechanism. Evaluation fees are not refunded on rule violations.

## Trading Restrictions

| Rule | Policy |
| --- | --- |
| Instruments | Forex pairs, commodities, indices, cryptocurrencies (platform-dependent) |
| Lot sizes | No restriction stated |
| EAs / Bots | Permitted for trade execution and risk management only; EAs that generate prohibited strategies (e.g., exploiting latency, copy farming) result in account termination |
| News trading | Permitted during evaluation; on funded accounts, profits from trades within 5 minutes of a high-impact news event are disqualified from payout calculations |
| Weekend holding | Allowed on funded accounts with the same news proximity restriction applied on Monday open |
| Hedging | Strictly prohibited on all account types during both evaluation and funded phases |
| Copy trading | Not specified as prohibited; signal farming (multiple accounts on the same signal) is prohibited |

## Consistency Rules

No formal consistency rule is stated for the 2-Step Classic or 1-Step models. However, strategies that generate a disproportionate share of profits from a single day or a small number of trades may be reviewed.

The 2-Step Pro model has a minimum of 1 trading day per phase, which implicitly discourages single-session trading.

## Scaling Plan

Funding Pips does not publish a formal scaling plan for funded accounts. Traders can hold up to the account's maximum position limits, and payout frequency choices (weekly/bi-weekly/monthly) affect the split percentage (60% / 80% / 100% respectively).

## Additional Rules

- Commissions: $2 per round lot on Forex, metals, and energies; other instruments are commission-free.
- Leverage: Up to 1:100 on 2-Step models; 1:50 on Zero and 1-Step models.
- Minimum payout: 1% of the initial account balance per withdrawal request.
- Funded accounts must maintain at least one active trade per 30 days to avoid inactivity closure (verify current policy on official website).

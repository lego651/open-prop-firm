# Funding Pips — Overview

Funding Pips is a proprietary trading evaluation firm founded in 2022 and headquartered in Dubai, UAE (IFZA Business Park). The firm offers CFD evaluation programs across four challenge models, catering to traders who prefer different risk/reward structures and evaluation speeds.

Funding Pips provides access to Forex pairs, commodities, indices, and cryptocurrencies through its evaluation platform. Account sizes range from $5,000 to $100,000. All trading is conducted in a simulated environment, with no real capital at risk during the evaluation phase.

## Challenge Models

Funding Pips offers four distinct evaluation models. Traders select their preferred model at purchase.

### Zero Challenge

An instant-funded model with no evaluation phase required. Traders begin trading immediately, subject to a 5% max drawdown and 3% daily loss limit.

### 1-Step Evaluation

A single-phase evaluation with a 10% profit target. Features tighter drawdown rules (3% daily, 6% overall) and a minimum of 3 trading days.

### 2-Step Classic

A two-phase evaluation with targets of 8% (Phase 1) and 5% (Phase 2). Maximum drawdown is 10%, daily limit is 5%. Most permissive drawdown structure.

### 2-Step Pro

A two-phase evaluation with targets of 6% per phase. Tighter parameters (3% daily, 6% overall) in exchange for a lower entry price.

## Key Features

- Payout splits scale with withdrawal frequency: 60% (weekly), 80% (bi-weekly), 100% (monthly)
- No time limit on any evaluation model
- Challenge fee is not explicitly refundable (payout split structure replaces fee refund)
- Commissions of $2 per round lot on Forex, metals, and energies; other instruments commission-free
- Leverage up to 1:100 on 2-Step Classic and Pro; 1:50 on Zero and 1-Step

# Lucid Trading — Trading Rules

Lucid Trading applies a consistent rule framework across all account types (LucidFlex, LucidPro, LucidDirect) with some differences by account variant in consistency requirements. All accounts use End-of-Day (EOD) trailing drawdown — the drawdown threshold only adjusts at the close of each trading session, not in real-time during the session.

## Drawdown Rules

- **Trailing drawdown (all accounts):** EOD trailing. The drawdown threshold trails upward based on the account's closing equity at the end of each session. Intraday drawdowns do not raise the threshold during the session — only the end-of-session equity triggers an update.
- **How EOD trailing works:** If the $25K account closes a session at $26,000, the trailing threshold rises accordingly. The threshold cannot decrease once set. If the account then drops to the threshold at any point (intraday or at close), the evaluation ends.
- **Drawdown amounts:** Vary by account size and variant — see individual account pages for specifics.

## Trading Restrictions

| Rule | Policy |
| --- | --- |
| EAs / Bots | Permitted; HFT (high-frequency trading) bots are restricted |
| News trading | Allowed on all account types during evaluation and funded phases |
| Weekend holding | Not permitted; swing trading is not allowed (verify current policy on official website) |
| Hedging | Not specified as prohibited; verify on official website |
| Copy trading | Allowed |
| Scalping | Permitted; at least 50% of profits must come from trades held longer than 5 seconds |

## Consistency Rules

Consistency requirements vary by account type and phase:

| Account | Evaluation Phase | Funded Phase |
| --- | --- | --- |
| LucidFlex | 50% — no single day can account for more than 50% of total profits | No consistency rule |
| LucidPro | No consistency rule | 40% — no single day can exceed 40% of total profits |
| LucidDirect | No evaluation phase | 20% — no single day can exceed 20% of total profits |

## Scaling Plan

Lucid Trading does not publish a formal scaling plan with preset milestones. The maximum funded allocation is $750,000 across up to 5 accounts per household. Payout structure includes 100% of profits on the first $10,000 in lifetime earnings per trader, then 90% thereafter.

## Additional Rules

- Inactivity rule: at least one trade must be placed every 30 calendar days to keep the account active.
- Payout processing: 3 business days for both LucidPro and LucidFlex funded accounts.
- Available payout methods: ACH, Wire, Plaid, Crypto.
- No activation fee or monthly fee on any account type.

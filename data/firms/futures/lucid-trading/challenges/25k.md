## Additional Details

- LucidDirect (instant-funded): $340, no evaluation phase, 20% consistency rule on funded account, $1,250 drawdown.
- No activation fee, no monthly fee on any variant.
- Scalping permitted; at least 50% of profits must come from trades held longer than 5 seconds.
- News trading is allowed on all account types during both evaluation and funded phases.

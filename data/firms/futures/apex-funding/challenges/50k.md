## How Trailing Drawdown Works

- **EOD type:** The $2,000 trailing threshold adjusts at end-of-day based on closing equity. Intraday drawdowns (within the session) do not raise the threshold.
- **Intraday type:** The $2,000 threshold trails in real-time. Every new equity high immediately raises the threshold floor.

Starting from $50,000: the initial trailing threshold is $48,000. If the account reaches $53,000 at end-of-day (EOD), the threshold rises to $51,000 and cannot go below that level.

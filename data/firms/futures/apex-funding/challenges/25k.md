## How Trailing Drawdown Works

- **EOD type:** The $1,000 trailing drawdown threshold is reset at the end of each trading day. Intraday equity gains do not lower your safety net in real-time — only the closing equity counts. This provides more intraday flexibility.
- **Intraday type:** The $1,000 threshold trails in real-time from the account's all-time peak equity. As the account grows, the threshold rises immediately.

For example, on the $25K EOD account: if the account starts at $25,000, the drawdown threshold is initially $24,000. After the account reaches $25,500 at end-of-day, the threshold rises to $24,500 and cannot go lower. If the account subsequently drops to $24,500, the evaluation ends.
